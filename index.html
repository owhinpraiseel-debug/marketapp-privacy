Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MarketApp ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MarketApp collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Payment card details — we store only the last 4 digits of your card number, expiry date, and cardholder name. Full card numbers are never stored.
  • Marketplace listings you create, including item name, price, description, category, and photos you choose to upload.
  • Grocery and budget data you enter manually.

2. How We Store Your Data

All sensitive data (including card details) is encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption via the Android Keystore system before being saved to your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Camera & Photos

MarketApp requests camera access solely to allow you to scan payment cards and take photos for marketplace listings. Camera images are processed locally on your device using Google ML Kit. No images are uploaded or stored remotely.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your full payment card numbers
  • We do not transmit any personal data to external servers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not share your data with third parties

5. Third-Party Services

MarketApp uses Google ML Kit (on-device text recognition) to scan card information from photos. This processing happens entirely on your device. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about ML Kit.

6. Children's Privacy

MarketApp is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by going to Settings → Apps → MarketApp → Clear Data on your Android device. Uninstalling the app also removes all associated data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: marketapp.support@gmail.com
